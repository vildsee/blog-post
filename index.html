Tromsø

Tromsø is the biggest city in North Norway. The town is located in two mountains separated by a river and connected by a bridge. It has amazing views, unique Artic church and of course the northen lights!

Hammerfest

Hammerfest is a quiet little town in North Norway. In the summer you can see lots of reindeers roaming around in the city and sun that doesn't set for two months. In the winter there is lots of snow and breathtaking aurora borealis aka the northen lights.
