Discovering Land of the Midnight Sun

Oslo and it's amazing surroundings

There is lot to discover in Oslo and around. In this post I am focusing more on the surroundings of Oslo, but I would like to reccomend visiting Norsk Folkemuseum in Oslo. It's an open air museum with different buildings, houses etc from different eras of Norway. There is also one wooden church Gol Stavekirke which looks amazing and smells good! :D

In and around Oslo there are a lot of amazing hiking trails! Norway has a freedom to roam law allemannsrett which means that everyone can enjoy the right of access to, and passage trough, uncultivated land in the countryside.
