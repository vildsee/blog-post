Tromsø - the Capital of the Arctic

August 2021

Tromsø is the biggest city in Norway that is located above the Arctic circle with about 65 000 people. When in Tromsø one should definetly visit the beautiful Telegrafbukta beach, you can't miss the Arctic Church and behind the church is a zip line you can take! The nature around is powerful and magical! Keep your eyes open when walking around in the city as it is filled with beautiful wall art!
